Accessing analytics in the Developer Portal
Last Updated: 2024-06-25
How to view your analytics data from the Developer Portal.

Viewing organization scoped analytics data
Click the arrow next to your consumer organization name on the Developer Portal home page, and click My organization.
Click the Analytics tab.
From the Analytics tab, you can see your organization analytics, which include: API statistics, total calls, and total errors.
Viewing application scoped analytics data
Click Apps from the Developer Portal dashboard.
Click the name of the application for which you want to view analytics data.
From the Dashboard tab, you can see your application analytics, which include: API statistics, total calls, and total errors.
